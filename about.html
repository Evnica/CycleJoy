About Bike Your Brain

The Idea

CityBike Wien provides around 1500 free city bikes at 121 stations. You have to register once with your credit card to make use of the Citybike services. The one-time registration fee is € 1.00. The first hour of every ride is always for free, the next hour is € 1.00. You can park the bike at one of the 121 stations and use another bike for the next ride.

For more information about City Bike Vienna click the link below: Vienna city bike

All along the tour you will stop in the best places of Vienna. If you reach a sight the app will give you a description about the sight. You can decide if you visit the Museum or just discover the sight and neighborhood from the outside. Once you have explored this place you have to answer a question, which is only available once you've reached read the description and are close to the point. Is the answer right you will get the coordinates to your new location and the route to the next sight is calculated. On the way you also have the possibility to activate the editor mode and create your own points of interest or edit already existing points to show other users places you like.

IMPORTANT: If you do no permit access to your current location all sights of the chosen trip are shown in the map and the route will not be calculated. You can do the trip without taking part in the quiz challenge!

Location:

The Mobile Application and the service are intended for use by users who access the application in the city of Vienna. If you give access to your location you can take part in the Quiz challenge. The user’s location is used to calculate the closest point of interest and to calculate the shortest route to this point. If you do not give access to your location the whole trip with all points of interest will be shown.
