About Bike Your Brain

The idea

Do you want to cycle in Vienna and learn more about the city? Then this app is for you! We offer you 3 thematic trips: nightlife journey, kids trip and a cultural challenge. Choose the one you like in the header on this page and get redirected to the map. You will see your first target. Navigate to it, read its description, answer a quiz question and find out, where you go next! Be careful: if you give a wrong answer twice, your next location might not be the closest one, and your trip may take longer than stated in the description. But don't worry! Even in this case you can still reach the final destination. Good luck!


IMPORTANT: If you do no permit access to your current location all sights of the chosen trip are shown in the map and the route will not be calculated. You can still do the trip, but without taking part in the quiz challenge.

NOTE: The app is in the demo mode now. This means that you can participate in the quiz without being located anywhere near the current destination. Once the test phase is over, the app quiz will be only accessible if you are located within 250m from the current target. Also, the geolocation feature and the corresponding control will be displayed only to the players located in Vienna.

If you need a rental bike

CityBike Wien provides around 1500 free city bikes at 121 stations. You have to register once with your credit card to make use of the Citybike services. The one-time registration fee is € 1.00. The first hour of every ride is always for free, the next hour is € 1.00. You can park the bike at one of the 121 stations and use another bike for the next ride.

For more information about City Bike Vienna click the link below: Vienna city bike

Location:

The Mobile Application and the service are intended for use by users who access the application in the city of Vienna. If you give access to your location you can take part in the Quiz challenge.
