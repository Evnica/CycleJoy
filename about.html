About Bike Your Brain

The Idea

Bike Your Brain Explore Vienna by bike!

Cycling is a fun, efficient and easy way of getting around in the city of Vienna. With this bike challenge app you can choose between 3 different trips: cultural trip, children’s trip or for those who want to get involved in Vienna’s nightlife, the nightlife trip!

The Team

This application is developed by students of the University of applied sciences Carinthia within the Master program 'Spatial Information Management. This is a project of the course 'aplied internet technologies'. Our task was to develop our 'first' web application with integerated maps.

The following students developed this application: Berk Buyükoglu, Judith Stratmann and Dariia Strelnikova.
