WELCOME to BIKE your BRAIN

Short Introduction

Explore Vienna by bike!
Cycling is a fun, efficient and easy way of getting around in the city of Vienna. With this bike challenge app you can choose between 3 different trips: cultural trip, children’s trip or for those who want to get involved in Vienna’s nightlife, the nightlife trip!

To find out more about the project click here.

Nightlife Trip

Vienna's nightlife scene is all about traditional cuisine and parties with music of the highest quality. There is something for everyone in this trip. From trendy to traditional bars with local food and lounge clubs with a stunning view over Vienna.


Duration: 5 sights (food & clubs)
Best starting time: 19:00 - 20:00
Price: Depending on your food & drinks
Age: 18 - 35

Kids Trip

From music to dance, from architecture and history, Vienna is the cultural city to explore! This trip brings you to famous buildings, unique museums and palaces. For all music, art & history lovers who want to learn about Austria’s capital should make this trip!


Duration: 5 sights (~7 hrs/12km)
Best starting time: between 9:00 - 12:00
Entrance fees: ~ 30 € p.p. (if you visit the museums)
Age: 8 - 14 years + parents

Cultural Trip

From music to dance, from architecture and history, Vienna is the cultural city to explore! This trip brings you to famous buildings, unique museums and palaces. For all music, art & history lovers who want to learn about Austria’s capital should make this trip!


Duration: 5 sights (~6 hrs)
Best starting time: between 9:00 - 12:00
Entrance fees: ~ 25 € p.p. (if you visit the museums)
Age: 16 - 80
