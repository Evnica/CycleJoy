WELCOME to BIKE your BRAIN

Short Introduction

Bike Your Brain CityBike Wien provides around 1500 free city bikes at 121 stations. You have to register once with your credit card to make use of the Citybike services. The one-time registration fee is € 1.00. The first hour of every ride is always for free, the next hour is € 1.00. You can park the bike at one of the 121 stations and use another bike for the next ride.

For more information about City Bike Vienna click the link below: https://www.citybikewien.at/en/news

All along the tour you will stop in the best places of Vienna. If you reach a sight the app will give you a description about the sight. You can decide if you visit the Museum or just discover the sight and neighborhood from the outside. Once you have explored this place you have to answer a question. Is the answer right you will get the coordinates to find the new location. With the use of an integrated route calculator you can insert the new coordinates and calculate the direction to the next sight. On the way you also have the possibility to activate the editor mode and create your own points of interest to show other users places you like.

After completing the challenge you have the possibility to get a certificate and if you give access your name will be stored on the Wall of Fame!

IMPORTANT: If you do not access your location, it is not possible to take part in the quiz challenge! If you do no permit access to your current location all sights of the chosen trip are shown in the map and the route will not be calculated.

Nightlife Trip

Vienna's nightlife scene is all about traditional cuisine and parties with music of the highest quality. There is something for everyone in this trip. From trendy to traditional bars with local food and lounge clubs with a stunning view over Vienna.

Duration: 5 sights (food & clubs)

Best starting time: 19:00 - 20:00

Price: Depending on your food & drinks

Age: 18 - 35

Kids Trip

From music to dance, from architecture and history, Vienna is the cultural city to explore! This trip brings you to famous buildings, unique museums and palaces. For all music, art & history lovers who want to learn about Austria’s capital should make this trip!

Duration: 5 sights (~7 hrs/12km)
Best starting time: between 9:00 - 12:00
Entrance fees: ~ 30 € p.p. (if you visit the museums)
Age: 8 - 14 years + parents
